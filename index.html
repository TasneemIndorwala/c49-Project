Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camel's ears are furry
  • Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet.
  • When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons live in warm habitats that range from rainforest to desert conditions.
  • Chameleons are adapted for climbing and visual hunting.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are gentle, plump, small-billed birds with a skin saddle between the bill and forehead.
  • Pigeons occur worldwide except in the coldest regions and the most remote islands.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Teddy-bears

  • Teddy-bears are owned by many children.
  • Teddy-bears are fluffy.
  • Teddy-bears are of many colors like pink, blue, red, yellow, etcetera.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Pandas have black and white fur.
  • Pandas are endangered animals.
  • Pandas love to eat bamboos.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas are large insects made conspicuous by the courtship calls of the males.
  • Cicadas have prominent eyes set wide apart, short antennae, and membranous front wings.
  • Cicadas are feeble jumpers, and nymphs lack the ability to jump altogether.
